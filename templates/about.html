About RemakePdf

Your trusted PDF toolkit for all document needs

Our Mission

RemakePdf was created with a simple goal: to provide everyone with free, fast, and secure PDF tools. We believe that working with PDF documents should be easy and accessible to all, without the need for expensive software or complicated processes.

What We Offer

Our comprehensive suite of 20+ PDF tools allows you to:

  • Merge, split, and organize PDF pages
  • Convert between PDF and other formats (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, JPG)
  • Compress PDFs to reduce file size
  • Rotate, crop, and edit PDF pages
  • Add watermarks, signatures, and protection
  • Extract text and images from PDFs
  • Perform OCR on scanned documents

Privacy & Security First

Your privacy is our top priority. All files are processed locally on our servers and are automatically deleted within 30 minutes. We never store, share, or access the content of your documents. Your files remain completely private and secure.

100% Free

All our PDF tools are completely free to use with no hidden fees, no registration required, and no limits on usage. We believe that essential document tools should be available to everyone.
