Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

At RemakePdf, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our PDF tools and services.

File Processing & Storage

We want to be completely transparent about how we handle your files:

  • Temporary Storage: Files you upload are stored temporarily only for the duration needed to process your request.
  • Automatic Deletion: All uploaded and processed files are automatically deleted from our servers within 30 minutes.
  • No Access: We do not access, read, or analyze the content of your documents.
  • No Sharing: Your files are never shared with third parties.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics such as which tools are used most frequently.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and IP address for security and service improvement.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to maintain session functionality.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our PDF tools
  • Improve user experience and service quality
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize performance

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Automatic file deletion systems
  • No permanent storage of user documents

Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information or files with third parties. Our PDF processing is done entirely on our own servers without external API calls.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we collect about you
  • Request deletion of any data we may have
  • Opt out of non-essential cookies
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
